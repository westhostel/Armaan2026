What is Armaan?
Armaan is the grand annual finale — the culmination of a year of academic, cultural, literary, and sporting events. It is where talent meets tradition, and the hostel's collective spirit shines brightest.

Event Highlights
Cultural performances spanning Odissi to contemporary fusion. Year-end awards for sports, literature & academics. Heritage magazine release. DJ night, live band, dance, and grand secret surprises.
